This is

Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play 5v5 tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games. It combines precise gunplay with unique character abilities. Players choose “Agents,” each with special skills that can change the course of a match. The main goal is to plant or defuse the spike, or eliminate the enemy team. Teamwork, strategy, and aim are the keys to winning.

Empowered by an unstoppable DNA mutation, Senegalese enforcer Veto defies the rules of engagement by nullifying his opponent's powers and technology. On Veto's battlefield, gunplay is your only guarantee.



HALL OF FAME

NRG

NRG Esports is a gaming organization from the U.S. founded in 2015. They compete in games like Valorant, Rocket League, Fortnite, and more. They are well-known for strong teams, big tournament wins, and popular gaming content.

Paper Rex

Paper Rex (PRX) is a Singaporean esports team famous in Valorant for their fast and aggressive gameplay. They are one of the top teams in the world and have many fans.

Sentinels

Sentinels is a professional Valorant esports team from North America (USA). They are one of the most popular and influential teams in the Valorant competitive scene. They gained massive attention after winning the VCT 2021 Masters Reykjavík, the first international Valorant LAN tournament, going undefeated.

Fnatic

Fnatic is a European professional Valorant esports organization, known for being one of the strongest and most consistent teams in the competitive scene. They became champions multiple times in international tournaments and are known for their structured and tactical gameplay.

PISTOL

Classic

The Classic is the free pistol that every player starts with in Valorant. Even though it costs nothing, it is still a useful and reliable weapon in many situations. It has two firing modes: a single-shot mode for accurate shots, and a burst-fire mode that shoots three bullets at once for close-range fights.

Sheriff

The Sheriff is a strong and powerful pistol in Valorant. It costs more than the other sidearms, but it makes up for it with high damage. One headshot from the Sheriff can kill an enemy, even if they have full armor. This makes the weapon very dangerous in the hands of a player with good aim.

Frenzy

The Frenzy is a fast-shooting pistol in Valorant that works best in close-range fights. It fires bullets very quickly, making it feel like a small SMG. Because of its high fire rate, the Frenzy can kill enemies fast, especially during pistol rounds or when rushing.

SMG

Spectre

Stable and mobile SMG. Accurate while moving, ideal for mid-close range and fast-paced fights.

Bulldog

Affordable rifle with a burst-fire option when zoomed. Good for eco rounds and mid-range fights.

Stinger

Lightweight SMG with very high fire rate. Works well in close-range fights but less accurate at long distances.

RIFLE

Vandal

High-damage rifle with consistent one-tap headshots at any distance. Very strong in skilled hands but less forgiving on missed shots.

Phantom

Silenced rifle with moderate recoil. Great for spraying at close-mid range and stealthy plays.

Guardian

Semi-auto rifle that excels at long-range precision. One-tap headshots make it ideal for careful shooters.

SNIPER

Marshal

Cheap sniper with one-shot headshots. Deals low body damage, so precision is key.

Operator

Powerful sniper capable of killing in one shot to the body or head at most ranges. Very effective for holding long angles.

Outlaw

Semi-automatic sniper rifle. Fires fast for a sniper, deals high damage, and is great for mid-to-long-range fights. Less powerful than the Operator, but allows faster follow-up shots.

SHOTGUN

Bucky

Semi-auto shotgun with high damage up close. Can spray in tight areas or switch to single shots for precision.

Judge

Fully automatic shotgun. Excellent for holding tight spaces or spraying through corners.

Shorty

A tiny shotgun pistol that’s deadly up close. Best for tight corners and surprise attacks.
